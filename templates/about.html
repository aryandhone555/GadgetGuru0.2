About Our Project

Our project 'GadgetGuru' is a smart AI-based recommendation system that assists users in choosing the best laptops based on their specific needs using NLP and Machine Learning. It simplifies the decision-making process for non-technical users by recommending laptops based on real-time data and user-friendly chatbot interactions.

Research Publication

Our research paper titled "Smart Laptop Recommender System Using NLP and Machine Learning" has been published in the International Research Journal of Modernization in Engineering Technology and Science (IRJMETS), November 2024 edition. In this work, we proposed an intelligent recommendation system that leverages Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning algorithms to understand user needs and suggest the most suitable laptops based on technical requirements, preferences, and market availability. This system is designed especially for non-technical users to simplify their purchase decisions through an intuitive and efficient chatbot-based interface.
