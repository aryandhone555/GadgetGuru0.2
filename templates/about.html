About Our Project

GadgetGuru is an intelligent, AI-powered laptop recommendation system designed to guide users in selecting the most appropriate laptops based on their usage patterns, technical requirements, and budget constraints. The system analyzes a wide range of factors, including hardware specifications, performance benchmarks, and real-time market availability. What sets GadgetGuru apart is its focus on simplifying the laptop selection process for users who may not have a technical background. By intelligently interpreting user inputs and comparing them against live data from online marketplaces, the system delivers tailored suggestions that align with both the user's needs and current trends in the tech market. This makes GadgetGuru a reliable virtual assistant for anyone looking to make informed and confident purchasing decisions.

Research Publication

Our research paper titled "Smart Laptop Recommender System Using AI and Machine Learning" has been published in the International Research Journal of Modernization in Engineering Technology and Science (IRJMETS), November 2024 edition. In this study, we introduced a smart recommendation engine that uses advanced machine learning techniques to evaluate user requirements—such as performance expectations, portability, and price sensitivity—and correlate them with a dynamically updated dataset of laptops sourced from various online platforms. The core objective of this system is to empower users, especially those with limited technical knowledge, by providing clear, relevant, and up-to-date laptop suggestions. The system ensures accuracy and reliability by continuously learning from new data, making it adaptive to evolving market conditions and user trends. This innovation demonstrates how AI can bridge the gap between consumer intent and technical product selection in an accessible and efficient manner.
